Open AppStart Journal
Platform mission

Trading improvement needs clean evidence.

SwanXm Trade Book is designed to turn every manual or synced trade into structured data: strategy, session, psychology, risk, result, and the next action.

Why it exists

Most traders lose track of the reason behind the result.

The platform fixes that by connecting journaling discipline with performance analytics. Instead of remembering only wins and painful losses, traders see the repeatable patterns behind both.

  • Capture manual trades quickly with setup, risk, session, and emotion tags.
  • Prepare secure MetaTrader 5 read-only syncing for automatic trade history import.
  • Review best day, best time, best session, best symbol, and best strategy.
  • Separate profitable behavior from overtrading, revenge trading, and news noise.
  • Build paid access tiers around automation, reports, and advanced analytics.

Product principles

Premium, precise, trader-first.

Clarity

Dashboards should answer the questions traders actually ask after a losing week.

Security

MT5 integration must use read-only access and server-side encryption in production.

Discipline

The journal rewards clean process, not random trade volume or emotional risk.
